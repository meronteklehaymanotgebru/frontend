About Us

Our Story

At Snack World, we’re more than a restaurant—we’re a celebration of Tigrayan identity. Founded in Axum, our mission is to share the warmth of our culture through food. From the communal injera platter to the aromatic spices of our kitchen, every dish tells a story of tradition and resilience. Visit us to taste the soul of Tigray.
